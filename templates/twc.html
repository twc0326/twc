ABOUT US

"Quality with a healthier choice" has always been our motto when we open our first retail outlet in the year 2017 and has always been since.

For over 20 years, we have been importing fresh vegetables as well as fruits (seasonal & year round) directly from farms both locally in West Malaysia as well as overseas' farms to local retail stores, fresh market and hypermarket.

As the years went by, we noticed that the number of retail stores that sells fresh fruits and vegetables have decreased and as such from the year 2017, the team decided to launch "Farmers Green House".

Our core vision is to ensure that you can have unparalleled fresh as well as quality fruits and vegetables.

Concurrently we strive to ensure the best shopping experience both online and offline. Whether it is in our physical retail outlets or through e-commerce platforms i.e. Grab Mart.
